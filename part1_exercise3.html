Part 1: QM/MM Simulations

Exercise 2 - Gas Phase Reaction

The next step is to calculate the reaction energies for going from chorismate to prephenate in the gas-phase. To do this, you must extract the coordinates of the substrate from the minimised conformations obtained from exercise 1 (which means that the simulation run in exercise 1 must have finished successfully before you can perform this exercise!)

Do this by running the command; 

sh get_qmonly

Run energy calculations of QM atoms only along the profile using the command; 

./charmm < qmonly_ener.inp > qmonly_ener.log
(This will take less than a minute)
