Part 2: Analysis of the Results

Exercise 3 - Looking for Correlation

From Part 2, exercise 2 you calculated a stabilisation energy. You also calculated the barrier height of the reaction (the relative energy at the transition state in Part 2, exercise 1). The final part of this practical is to examine whether or not there is any correlation of these values.

Open the spreadsheet file correlation.xls. This is a list of barrier heights and stabilisation energies from several other snapshots from the original molecular dynamics simulation. Copy the values from this spreadsheet into your spreadsheet. Add the barrier height and stabilisation energy that you calculated to this list, then plot stabilisation energy versus barrier height. Is there any correlation? Calculate the correlation coefficient (R^2). What does any correlation or lack of correlation imply? How well do your results fit with the results from other snapshots?
