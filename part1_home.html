Part 1: QM/MM Simulations

We will be using a molecular dynamics program called CHARMM. CHARMM is not a nice and friendly program, but it is very powerful, and is one of the few programs that is capable of performing the QM/MM simulations that we need to perform to investigate enzyme catalysis. We shall also be using the VMD molecular visualisation program to view the structure of the enzyme and to follow the reaction graphically.

In these exercises you will use CHARMM and VMD to model the enzyme catalysed reaction of chorismate to prephenate in the enzyme, and will then compare this reaction by running simulations of the reaction in the gas-phase. We shall use VMD to look at the changes in structure of the enzyme during the reaction, and shall also see how the simulations can be used to quantify the catalytic power of the enzyme.
