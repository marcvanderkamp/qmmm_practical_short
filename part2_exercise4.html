Exercise 4 - Gas phase reaction with bound solitary Arg90 (QM)

In exercises 1 and 2, you calculated stabilisation energies by comparing the reaction profile of the reaction of chorismate to prephenate in the enzyme against the reaction in the gas phase. To provide a similar comparison for the reaction of chorismate to prephenate in the enzyme with a QM Arg90, it is necessary to calculate the reaction profile of the gas phase reaction bound next to a solitary QM Arg90.

Extract the total energies of the reactant, transition state and product via

grep "ENER QUANTM>" <output_file_name>.log

(where <output_file_name>.log is the name of the output files you have used in part 1, exercise 4, e.g. qm_r90qm_-1.8.log). Place these energies into your spreadsheet and calculate relative energies as in Part 2, exercise 1. Plot these relative energies on the graph produced in Part 2, exercise 1.

Calculate the stabilisation energy (gas-phase with QM Arg90 relative energy minus enzyme with QM Arg90 relative energy). Plot the stabilisation energy on the graph you produced in Part 2, exercise 2.
