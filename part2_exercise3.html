Exercise 3 - Enzyme Catalysed Reaction with QM Arg90

In exercise 1 of the first session you simulated the reaction of chorismate to prephenate while bound to the enzyme chorismate mutase.

In exercise 2 of the first session you repeated the simulation, but this time, one of the residues of chorismate mutase (Arg90) was modelled using QM, rather than MM.

In this exercise you will calculate the reaction profile when Arg90 in chorismate mutase is modelled using QM.

Extract the total energy of the reactant, transition state and product structures by using 

grep "ENER>" qmmm_r90qm_reactant.log

grep "ENER>" qmmm_r90qm_ts.log

grep "ENER>" qmmm_r90qm_product.log

Place the energies in your spreadsheet, and calculate the relative energies, as in part 2, exercise 1. Plot the relative energies on the same graph you produced in part 2, exercise 1.
